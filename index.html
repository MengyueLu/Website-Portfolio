About Me

I am a dynamic and creative writer with a Bachelor's degree in English and Creative Writing from Baylor University. My specialization in narrative development and script editing for the gaming industry has honed my ability to craft compelling dialogues and rich character arcs. I have technical proficiency in Ren'Py, Twine, and Godot, which I utilize to enhance user engagement and deliver immersive storytelling experiences in interactive media. During my career, I have led the development and writing of choice-based visual novels, managing all aspects of project development from planning and scripting to iterative testing. My work on projects like "Cascade Pines" and "Of Mice and Vices" demonstrates my capability to develop branching storylines and complex character relationships that significantly impact the plot based on player choices.

My internships and outside projects have provided me with hands-on experience in corporate communications and visual storytelling, allowing me to effectively communicate complex ideas and collaborate with senior management to refine strategies. I am recognized for my ability to enhance narrative coherence and depth, ensuring high-quality outcomes in storytelling.

I am passionate about contributing innovative narrative solutions to the gaming industry, advancing game development, and elevating player immersion through meticulous storytelling and interactive experiences.
